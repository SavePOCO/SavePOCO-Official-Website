The Impact of the Right to Repair.

The impact of our Right to Repair on the environment and the community is immense, and almost immeasurable.

Every year, 50 million tonnes of e-waste is thrown into the environment around us, but we as a community can help stop this. We can work together, to make sure our voices are heard, to make an effort in reducing the amount of e-waste produced every year, and using the resources toward making repairability more accessible all over Ireland, and even the whole world.

The environment is our home, and we should do everything in our power to protect it, and to
