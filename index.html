About Us

Freepair Ireland's mission is to make repairing your devices as easy as possible.

The main focus of our initiative is to bring forward the issues of many industries, such as the automotive industry, technological industry, medical equipment and more. The main issue with most of these industries, is the lack of support for repairability. We are here to change that, once and for all.

To achieve our goals and complete our mission, Freepair Ireland have decided to take on the mantle of spreading the word about these issues. We believe everyone deserves to have knowledge regarding the Right to Repair.
