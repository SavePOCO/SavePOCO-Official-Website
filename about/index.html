About Us

We at Freepair Ireland believe in freedom.

The freedom we crave, however, is rather unspoken about. The freedom to repair your products is at our fingertips, but not quite there. This is because not everyone knows what our Right to Repair is, and that is why we are here. To simply promote it, and help wherever we can.

Our team at Freepair Ireland consists of 4 individuals, all passionate for the same thing; Having freedom to repair our products. Join us and our mission. Help us spread the message.

To find out more about the Right to Repair, visit Repair EU, the main source of information about your rights regarding repairing your devices as an individual.
